About LeonidaVice

LeonidaVice is an independent fan-built financial terminal for Grand Theft Auto VI. Think of it as a Bloomberg Terminal for criminals — a high-fidelity dashboard designed specifically for the world of GTA VI and the fictional State of Leonida.

Instead of being just another gaming news blog, LeonidaVice acts as a professional-grade utility that players keep open on a second monitor to track the in-game economy in real-time. By providing simulated "market data" on in-game stock fluctuations, property ROI calculators, and a live ticker of asset valuations, the platform turns the game's wealth-building mechanics into a data-driven experience.

Use cases range from timing stock buys around story-driven "assassination" missions to checking a live "Leonida Market News" feed for tips on which businesses are currently the most profitable to own in the online world.

Our Mission

We built LeonidaVice because we believe GTA VI's economy deserves the same analytical rigor that real-world traders apply to financial markets. The game's wealth-building mechanics are deep, nuanced, and genuinely strategic — and we want to give players the tools to engage with them at that level.

  • Utility over fluff — it's a tool you use while playing, not just a site you read once
  • The "Pro" aesthetic — it looks like high-end fintech software, not a messy fan wiki
  • The "Leonida" niche — focused on the specific vibe of GTA VI's setting
  • Data-driven gameplay — turning in-game decisions into informed, strategic choices

The Team

LeonidaVice was built by a small team of GTA enthusiasts and fintech developers who wanted to combine their two passions. We're not affiliated with Rockstar Games or Take-Two Interactive in any way.

  • Terminal Architecture — market simulation engine and real-time data feeds
  • UI/UX Design — the premium fintech aesthetic you see throughout the site
